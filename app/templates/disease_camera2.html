Crop:Mango
Disease: Xanthomonas axonus (Mango bacterial spot)
Cause of disease:
Mango bacterial spot is one of the most common diseases of mangoes in many areas where it is grown.
2. The most devastating efect of Mango bacterial spot occur in areas where it rains during the mango flowering and fruit set stages.
3. Bacterial growth is favoured by wet or humid weather. It is particularly favoured by rain and wind.

Cure for the disease:
1. Make nurseries for raising rootstocks and scion wood that are known to be free of disease.
2. Establish good windbrakes for the trees.
3. Harvest by clipping the fruit stalks, do not tear the fruit off the trees.
4. Wash hands with soap and water before and during the handling of plants to reduce potential spread between plants.
